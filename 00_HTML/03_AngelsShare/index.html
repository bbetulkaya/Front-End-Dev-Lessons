About The Tavern

Angel's Share is a tavern that can be found in the City of Mondstadt. A place where you can find delicate alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks. Our drinks are so famous that you can hear by all around the Teyvat.

The Nights at The Tavern

Nights when everyone gets away from their troubles while sipping their wine while the gentle melodies of lyre played in the background by the bard.

Tavern Trivia

Who is our loyal customer?

Venti the Bard!

He is a bard who, despite looking young, has a high tolerance for alcohol. According to Charles, the tavern owner Master Diluc said, " He can drinks many of wines if he plays his lyre and makes the customer happy ."
